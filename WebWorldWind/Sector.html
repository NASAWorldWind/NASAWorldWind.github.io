computeBoundingPoints(globe, verticalExaggeration) → {Vec3}

Returns an array of Vec3 that bounds the specified sector on the surface of the specified Globe. The returned points enclose the globe's surface terrain in the sector, according to the specified vertical exaggeration, minimum elevation, and maximum elevation. If the minimum and maximum elevation are equal, this assumes a maximum elevation of 10 + the minimum.
